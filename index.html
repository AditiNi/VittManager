All Charges

Equity Delivery

Charged by broker on buy orders when equity is delivered in dmat account. It is zero on VM.

Equity Intraday

Charged by brokes, if you buy and sell on the same day. It is charged at ₹20 or 0.05%(whichever is lower) per executed order.

Auto Square-off Charges

Charged by broker, on square-off(exit) of any open intraday position by system after 3:10PM. It is ₹50 +GST per position.

Account Opening Charges

Charged one time fo opening trading and demat account.It will be ₹200  ₹0(Limited Period Offer) on VM.

Transaction Charges

Charged by exchange for trading. It is 0.00325% on NSE and 0.003% on BSE of order amount, applicable on both buy and sell.

Stamp Duty

Charged by government as stamp duty for contract note. It is applicable on buy orders only-0.015% for equity deivery and 0.003% for equity intraday.

Clearing Charges

Charged by clearing member of NSSCL(clearing corporation of NSE) and ICCL(clearing corporation of BSE). It is Zero on VM.

GST

GST of 18% is applied on charges wherever applicable.

DP Charges(Sell Order)

Charged by DP for debiting stocks from emat account.It is ₹8 + ₹5.50(CDSL Charges) per ISIN(comapny/ETF) per day o VM regardless of quantity sold.

DP Charges(Buy Order)

Charged by DP for crediting stocks to demat account.It si Zero on VM.

SEBI Turnover Charges

Charged at 0.000005% by SEBI(Securities and Exchange Board of India) for regulating the markets.

Securities Transaction Tax(STT)

Charged by government when you transact on exchanges.It is 0.1% of order amount in case of equity delivery and 0.025% of order amount in case of equity intraday.

Paytm Gateway Charges

Charges for depositing money in VM. It is zero of now.
